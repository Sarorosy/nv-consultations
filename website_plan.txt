Multispecialty Consultations

At NV Consultations, we strive to provide comprehensive and coordinated healthcare. In addition to our primary services, specialist consultations are available based on patient needs.

Through our network of visiting consultants, patients can access expert care in the following specialties:

General Surgery

Surgical Oncology

Orthopedic Surgery

Plastic Surgery

ENT (Ear, Nose & Throat)

Nephrology

Urology


These consultations are available by prior appointment, ensuring patients receive timely specialist evaluation and guidance when required.

Our goal at NV Consultations is to offer convenient access to multidisciplinary medical expertise, enabling patients to receive comprehensive care under one roof.
