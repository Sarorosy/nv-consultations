Dr. R. Navin

Director & Consultant Diabetologist – NV Consultations, Kolathur, Chennai

Dr. R. Navin is a dedicated Consultant Diabetologist and Director of NV Consultations, with a strong focus on comprehensive diabetes care, preventive medicine, and long-term metabolic health management. With extensive clinical training and academic exposure in diabetes care, he is committed to providing evidence-based treatment tailored to individual patient needs.

Dr. Navin completed his MBBS from Government Medical College, Chengalpattu, and further enhanced his expertise through specialized training in diabetes management. He holds the Fellowship in Clinical Diabetology (FCD) from the Indian Medical Association (IMA) and advanced certifications including CCEBDM and CCGDM from the Public Health Foundation of India (PHFI).

He has also completed the Certificate Course in Diabetes Care from Leicester Diabetes Centre, UK, one of the globally recognized institutions for diabetes research and clinical training. In 2025, he completed the Enhanced Postgraduate Certification in Diabetes Mellitus (EPGCD) to further strengthen his expertise in advanced diabetes management.

Currently, Dr. Navin is pursuing M.Sc. in Advancing Diabetes Care from Birmingham City University, UK, reflecting his continued commitment to staying at the forefront of modern diabetes care.

Dr. Navin has a special interest in Diabetic Foot Care, and has undergone specialized training in diabetic foot procedures at the Institute of Medical and Minimal Access Surgery Training, Mumbai.

In addition to his work at NV Consultations, he also serves as a Visiting Consultant Diabetologist at Apollo Medical Centre, Valasaravakkam and Prashanth Super Speciality Hospital, Kolathur, and has previously consulted at multiple clinics in the region.

His dedication to improving diabetes care has been recognized with prestigious awards including:

RSSDI Innovation Award in Diabetes Care (2023)

Diabetes India Award for Excellence in Diabetes Care and Management (2024)


Dr. Navin is a Life Member of RSSDI, Indian Medical Association (IMA), and the Indian Physicians Association (IPA).

At NV Consultations, he focuses on a patient-centred approach combining clinical expertise, preventive care strategies, and long-term lifestyle management to help patients achieve better metabolic health and improved quality of life.

Dr. Varsha Ezhilarasu

Co-Director, Consultant Physician & Diabetologist – NV Consultations

Dr. Varsha Ezhilarasu is a Consultant Physician and Diabetologist at NV Consultations, dedicated to providing comprehensive, patient-centred medical care. She has a strong clinical interest in the management of diabetes, hypertension, thyroid disorders, and other lifestyle-related diseases, with a focus on early diagnosis, preventive healthcare, and long-term disease management.

She completed her MBBS from Government Chengalpattu Medical College and later pursued DNB in Anaesthesiology from MIOT Hospital, Chennai, where she gained valuable experience in anaesthesia and critical care.

To further strengthen her expertise in metabolic disorders, she completed CCEBDM (Certificate Course in Evidence Based Diabetes Management) from the Public Health Foundation of India and FCD – Fellowship in Clinical Diabetology, equipping her with advanced knowledge in modern diabetes care.

Dr. Varsha strongly believes in a holistic approach to patient care, emphasizing lifestyle modification, preventive medicine, and patient education as key components in the management of chronic diseases. She is actively involved in community health initiatives, diabetes awareness programs, preventive health camps, and patient education activities.

Her contributions to diabetes care have been recognized with the Diabetes India Award (2025).

She is a member of the Indian Medical Association (IMA) and the Research Society for the Study of Diabetes in India (RSSDI).

At NV Consultations, Dr. Varsha works towards empowering patients with the knowledge and tools needed to achieve better metabolic health and improved long-term wellbeing.
